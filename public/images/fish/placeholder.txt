Fish Images Directory

This directory contains fish species images for the Rhode Island Fishing Guide app.

For now, placeholder images are being used. To add real fish images:

1. Source images from public domain or Creative Commons sources:
   - U.S. Fish and Wildlife Service Digital Library
   - NOAA Photo Library
   - Wikimedia Commons
   - Public domain fish identification guides

2. Recommended image specifications:
   - Format: JPG or PNG
   - Size: 400x300 pixels minimum
   - Quality: High resolution for web display
   - License: Public domain or Creative Commons with attribution

3. Naming convention:
   - striped-bass.jpg
   - largemouth-bass.jpg
   - fluke.jpg
   - tautog.jpg
   - etc.

4. Required fish species images:
   - Striped Bass
   - Fluke (Summer Flounder)
   - Tautog (Blackfish)
   - Bluefish
   - Scup (Porgy)
   - Black Sea Bass
   - Winter Flounder
   - Weakfish (Squeteague)
   - Atlantic Cod
   - Pollock
   - Brown Trout
   - Rainbow Trout
   - Largemouth Bass
   - Smallmouth Bass
   - Chain Pickerel
   - Yellow Perch
   - White Perch
   - Bluegill
   - Pumpkinseed
   - American Eel
   - Brook Trout
   - Northern Pike
   - Carp

The app will gracefully handle missing images by displaying a fish icon placeholder.
